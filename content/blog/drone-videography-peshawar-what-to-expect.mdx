### Weddings and Events

Imagine your wedding venue revealed from a sweeping aerial shot — the lights, the decorations, the scale of the celebration all captured in one cinematic frame. Drone footage adds a premium, film-like quality to wedding videos that ground-level cameras simply cannot replicate.

- **Overhead formations** — Bird's-eye view of the stage, seating arrangements, and décor

## Legal Requirements in Pakistan

Before flying drones in Pakistan, it's essential to understand the regulatory framework set by the Pakistan Civil Aviation Authority (PCAA):

- **Registration** — All commercial drones must be registered with PCAA
- **Pilot licensing** — Drone operators need proper certification
- **Flight zones** — Certain areas (near airports, military installations, government buildings) are restricted
- **Altitude limits** — Commercial drone flights are typically limited to 120 meters above ground level
- **Permission** — Venue owners and event hosts should be informed
